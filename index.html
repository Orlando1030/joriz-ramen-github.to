Ramen

Ramen is a japanese dish. It consist of chinese style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish based broth,often flavored with soy sauce or miso,and uses topings such as sliced pork,nori,menma,and scallions


Did you know?

Momofuku Ando invents the instant noodles in Sapporo,Japan. Mr. Ando really outdid himself here. His parting gift to mankind was the “Space Ram,” a vacuum-packed ramen with smaller noodles and a thicker broth. Japanese astronaut Soichi Noguchi feasted upon these vac-packed instanoodles while aboard the Discovery space shuttle.
